Spaghetti Bridge Project

Your 90-Minute Guide to Design & Prototyping

Class Goals

By the end of this session, your team will have made significant progress toward the final project by achieving both cognitive and social-emotional goals.

Cognitive Goals

  • Apply learned concepts like tension, compression, and truss design to a real-world problem.
  • Develop a formal design brief and a detailed specification.
  • Practice critical thinking by analyzing different design options.

Social-Emotional (SEL) Goals

  • Collaborate effectively within a team of four.
  • Communicate design ideas and technical concepts clearly.
  • Demonstrate problem-solving and resilience when faced with design challenges.

Knowledge Check

Complete this quick check after watching the video. No peeking at the vocabulary cards!

  1. What force pulls a material apart? A force of __________.
  2. A force that pushes or squeezes a material together is called __________.
  3. What is the name for the sudden failure of a structural member under high compressive stress? __________.
  4. What kind of bridge uses a series of interconnected triangles to distribute weight? A __________ bridge.

Answer Key:

  • Tension
  • Compression
  • Buckling
  • Truss
