General Class Notes

  • Flexible Pace: This is a guide. We'll adjust the pace of the class to make sure everyone is learning and having fun.
  • Visual Resources: We'll use diagrams, short videos, and live demonstrations to help you understand the concepts.
  • Errors are Opportunities: Don't be afraid to make mistakes! They are part of the learning process. We'll work together to debug code and wiring.
  • Celebrate the Achievements: Every small success is a huge win! Whether it's a blinking LED or a moving square on the screen, we'll celebrate it.
  • Connect to Real Life: We'll always try to relate the concepts we learn to real-world applications.
  • Documentation: We'll get into the good habit of commenting our code and documenting our 3D designs.
