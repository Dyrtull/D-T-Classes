Activity 2: BUILD TIME (Crank & Slider)

Instructions:

  1. The Base: Place cardboard rectangle (20cm x 15cm) flat.
  2. The Crank: Pin the Circle (5cm) to the Base.
  3. The Rod: Pin the Strip (12cm) to the edge of the Circle.
  4. The Slider: Tape two straws parallel on the Base.
  5. Connect: Put the Rod end between the straws.
