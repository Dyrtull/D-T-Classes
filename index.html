About the Course

The IGCSE Design & Technology 0445 course is an exciting journey into the world of creative problem-solving and practical application. Throughout the year, you will learn to research, design, and create innovative solutions to real-world challenges.

This curriculum is designed to develop your understanding of materials, manufacturing processes, and the design cycle. We'll be using this website to keep all your resources in one place, making it easy to access at any time.
